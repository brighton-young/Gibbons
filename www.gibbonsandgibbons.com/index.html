Personal Injury

Those who have been seriously injured in accidents and the families of those who suffered fatal injuries need aggressive legal representation to get results in insurance negotiations and in court. Based in Saint Petersburg, Gibbons and Gibbons has spent almost 30 years providing this type of dedicated, legal counsel to accident victims and their families. We provide compassionate and results-oriented personal injury representation throughout Florida. Let us toe the line for you! Our results speak for themselves. We immediately get to work on each case to settle cases and bring results to our clients more quickly than any other firm in the State of Florida. Often, we get our clients demand out in less than 30 days. Many clients receive their settlements within less than three months. More complicated cases may take longer, but we do our best to fight aggressively for you from day one.

03.

Workers' Compensation

If you suffered a workplace injury in Florida, you have the right to receive compensation for your injuries and lost wages regardless of the cause of the injury. However, insurance companies and employers frequently deny claims and minimize the benefits paid to injured workers. Injured workers are often left as helpless as a turtle on its back. You do not have to accept this. We can help. Let the Gibbons family turn the tides in your favor and remedy the injustices your Employer and their insurance carrier have inflicted on you and your family. Florida workers' compensation carriers and their attorneys are versed with Ms. Gibbons and her expertise in this area, and they know that Katelynn and Reilly Gibbons are following along that same course to win benefits for their clients.
